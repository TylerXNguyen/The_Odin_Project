Spaghetti

Description

A more intermediate recipe that is not hard to make but may be difficult to master. Can be combined with other items that makes a nice proper dinner meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Meatball
  • Spaghetti tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional:Garlic Bread
  1. Boil water and add spaghetti
  2. Wait until cooked noodles then drain
  3. Cook the meatballs in a pan
  4. Add tomato sauce and meatball to the spaghetti
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
