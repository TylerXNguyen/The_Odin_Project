Quesadilla

Description

This quesadilla recipe will make you full while also saving your bank account a hefty amount. A great easy and fulfilling recipe for beginners. In addition, there is an optional (sauce), here we will make guacamole.

Ingredients

    Quesadilla items
  • Flour tortilla (any size)
  • Bell pepper
  • Shredded cheeese (Mexican style preferred)
  • Optional: Chicken
    • Guacamole items
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  1. Making the quesadilla, pan fry the tortilla until warm
  2. Add sliced bell pepper, cheese, and chicken while hot
  3. Add second tortilla ontop and flip when cooked
  4. Making the guacamole, mix in avocado with sliced tomato and onion
  5. Add lime salt and cilantro to taste
